Work

My distinctive illustrations have been used for everything from wine labels to children’s books, from portraits for Macy’s to maps for The New York Times. My career is centered around designing for real people, no matter the medium or platform.

Process

The loosest sketches often germinate my best ideas. Research and sketching form the base for polished drawings, made with everything near my fingertips: ink, paint, (more) toothbrushes, and a wacom tablet. The final art clearly reflects an illustrator’s hand, but is digital in presentation and flexibility.

About

With a background in visual journalism at Newsweek and an MFA in Illustration I am very comfortable in the intersection of words and images. (read more) Co-founding BumpBump Books led me into the realm of designing for mobile devices, while a recent degree in UX reinforced my love of research and human centered design.
